About Us

"Still, there are moments when one feels free from one's own identification with human limitations and inadequacies. At such moments, one imagines that one stands on some spot of a small planet, gazing in amazement at the cold yet profoundly moving beauty of the eternal, the unfathomable: life and death flow into one, and there is neither evolution nor destiny; only being."
- ALBERT EINSTEIN, letter to Queen Mother Elisabeth of Belgium, Jan. 9, 1939

What started to be a project, today is a real blog. Gaussian Lair is a place where i, Henrique Yuiti, post what i love the most: knowledge. I created this blog with the purpose to fullfill my necessity to share what i learned, other than that, authorized authors can also write their ideias. In the range of physics to literature, all i can tell is: "We won't have time to learn everything, and because of that sharing what we know can facilitate the way to make people realize how humanity can be awesome. It's time to live, to share, to create and imaginate!". With this phrase i finish my thoughts and for that, i'll invite you for making part of this wonderful journey.
